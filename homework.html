How To Make A Sandwich

  1. Buy a loaf of bread, cheese, and butter.
  2. Spread a moderate amount of butter on two slices of bread.
  3. Place one or more pieces of cheese on one piece of bread.
  4. Put the two pieces of bread together.
  5. Throw on grill if desired.
